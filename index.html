Ōri, also known as Arjun Venugopal, is an experimental electronic musician hailing from Kerala, India. With over a decade of experience creating music under different aliases, Ōri has established a unique sound that blends downtempo and post-rock influences with lush, melodic ambient soundscapes.

He released his first EP titled "Whispers" in 2016, followed by his second EP "Nowhere from Here" in 2019, post collaborations and singles. After a brief hiatus, his third EP "Happier Days" is set to release in February 2023.

He is also currently a member of the electronic duo "Parallel Highway".
